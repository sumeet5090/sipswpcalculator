# How to Reach $1 Million (or ₹1 Crore) in 18 Years with Mutual Funds
Hitting the million/crore milestone is a matter of clear math and consistency

---

<p class="text-xl mb-6">Reaching a $1 million or ₹1 Crore milestone is a dream for many investors. While it may seem daunting, it is a mathematical certainty if you have the right strategy and the discipline to stick to it over 18 to 20 years.</p>

<h2 class="text-2xl font-bold mt-8 mb-4">The Magic of Consistency</h2>
<p class="mb-6">The key to reaching a seven-figure milestone isn't finding the "next big stock"—it's consistency. By using a Systematic Investment Plan (SIP), you can automate your wealth building and take advantage of market dips through rupee-cost averaging.</p>

<h2 class="text-2xl font-bold mt-8 mb-4">The Roadmap to $1 Million</h2>
<p class="mb-6">Assuming an average annual return of 12%, here is what you would need to invest monthly to hit $1,000,000 in 18-to-20 years:</p>
<ul class="list-disc pl-6 mb-6 space-y-4">
    <li><strong>20-Year Horizon:</strong> Approximately <strong>$1,000 per month</strong>.</li>
    <li><strong>18-Year Horizon:</strong> Approximately <strong>$1,350 per month</strong>.</li>
    <li><strong>With a 10% Annual <a href="/resources#step-up-sip" class="text-indigo-600 hover:underline">Step-Up</a>:</strong> You can start with as little as <strong>$500 per month</strong> and still hit the million-dollar mark in 20 years.</li>
</ul>

<h2 class="text-2xl font-bold mt-8 mb-4">The Roadmap to ₹1 Crore</h2>
<p class="mb-6">For Indian investors, reaching the ₹1 Crore (10 million INR) mark is a significant milestone. At 12% returns:</p>
<ul class="list-disc pl-6 mb-6 space-y-4">
    <li><strong>20-Year Horizon:</strong> Approximately <strong>₹10,000 per month</strong>.</li>
    <li><strong>18-Year Horizon:</strong> Approximately <strong>₹13,500 per month</strong>.</li>
    <li><strong>With a 10% <a href="/resources#step-up-sip" class="text-indigo-600 hover:underline">Step-Up</a>:</strong> Starting with <strong>₹5,000 per month</strong> will get you there in 20 years.</li>
</ul>
